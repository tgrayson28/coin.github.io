Who is COIN?

Coin is an indie pop band from Nashville, TN
They formed in 2012, but did not gain much mainstream attention until 2016, when
the song "Talk Too Much" was released. Currently they have over 4 million listeners on spotify alone! Chase Lawrence is the lead vocalist, Ryan Winnen is the drummer,
and Joe Memmel is the lead guitarist.
